# About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Psychology at the University of Groningen.

My research interests focus on understanding human cognition and behavior. I am particularly interested in cognitive processes, social cognition, and individual differences.

My work aims to investigate psychological mechanisms underlying human thoughts, decisions, and behaviors using behavioral and quantitative research methods.


<span class='anchor' id='research'></span>

# Research Interests

My research interests include:

- Cognitive psychology
- Social cognition
- Individual differences
- Mental health and well-being
- Psychological mechanisms underlying human behavior

I am interested in exploring how cognitive and social factors shape human behavior and psychological outcomes.
