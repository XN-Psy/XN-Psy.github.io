# About Me

Hi, I am Ning Xu, a PhD candidate in Sociology at the University of Groningen. My doctoral research is conducted within the GUTS project under the supervision of Prof. D.R. Veenstra, Prof. B. Güroğlu, and Dr. T. Guthrie.

<span class='anchor' id='research'></span>

# Research Interests

My research interests focus on adolescent social development, particularly how peer relationships and social contexts shape adolescents' social behaviors and psychological adjustment. My research areas include:

- **School Bullying**: Bully-defenders;The healthy context paradox.
- **Peer relationship**: Examining how peer status, peer clique, friendship networks influence social behaviors and mental health.

My PhD research, as part of the GUTS project, integrates neurobiological perspectives with previous research on peer dynamics to further understand adolescent development.
